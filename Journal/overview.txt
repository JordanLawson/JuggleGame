GENERAL PROJECT INFO

Project Name: Juggle Gun
Started: 15 JUN 2018
Engine: Game Maker Studio 1.4 (YoYo Games)

Description:
Juggle Gun is a 2D platform game where the player uses various weapons to defeat opponnets.  The unique feature of the game is that weapons are only reloaded/reset when thrown away from the player, forcing them to constantly find new weapons or "juggle" the one they have.
